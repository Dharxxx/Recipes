lasagna

Lasagna is a type of pasta made of very wide flat sheets.Either term can refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers 
        of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices

Steps
  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
